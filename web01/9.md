### 4. 前台的顯示

```php
//t9
$re=select("t9_news","dpy=1 limit 5");
$t9more=(count($re)>4)?'<a style="float:right" href="news.php">More...</a>':'';
$t9txt='';
foreach($re as $ro) $t9txt.='<li>'.mb_substr($ro['text'],0,15).'<span class="all" style="display:none">'.$ro['text'].'</span></li>';
```
